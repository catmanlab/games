Gun Index

Damage scales with rarity (Common → Epic in chests; Legendary from red bosses; Mythic from signature bosses only). Every gun starts with 50 ammo. Press E to reload.

Assault rifle

Modulated AR

30 before reload · 1.5s reload · 12 bloom · Press F to switch mode

A dual-mode assault rifle that trades fire rate for punch. Press F at any time to swap modes (two-player same device: Player 2 uses V).

  • Rapid — Nearly Tactical Shotgun speed (~151 ms per shot at Rare). Lighter hits (~82% AR damage). Good for close pressure and finishing weak targets.
  • Heavy — Revolver-slow cadence (~403 ms per shot at Rare). Much harder hits (~128% AR damage). Best for mid-range taps and burst damage.

Balanced rifle range and spread — same effective distance as a standard Assault Rifle. Rare point-blank damage: Rapid ~37–53 per shot; Heavy ~58–83 per shot.

Found in: Brown chests (Common–Epic)

Shotgun

Overdrive Shotgun

20 before reload · 1.9s reload · 14 bloom

Mid/close-range shotgun that gets stronger as the mag runs dry. First shots are weak (~55% damage); last shells before reload hit hardest (~145% damage). Slightly fast fire — burn through the mag for peak damage, then reload.

Found in: Brown chests (Common–Epic)

SMG

Suppressed SMG

36 before reload · 2s reload · 7 bloom

Very high fire rate with strong damage and heavy ammo use. Short bursts stay accurate to mid range — ideal run-and-gun.

Found in: Brown chests (Common–Epic)

Assault rifle

Machine Gun

60 before reload · 3.25s reload · 8 bloom

Fast sustained fire with a huge magazine. Strong at close-to-mid range; bloom builds quickly — poor at long range. Great for shredding walls and pressure fights up close.

Found in: Brown chests (Common–Epic)
